Intelligence

It learns what works

Every merged PR teaches Claudear something. Local vector embeddings capture patterns. Review feedback gets internalized. Fix strategies that work get reinforced. No external APIs — all learning stays on your machine.

Collaboration

Asks when it's stuck

When Claude encounters ambiguity, it doesn't guess. It asks your team a question via Discord, Slack, or Email. The first reply wins. Claude picks up where it left off. No context lost.

Vigilance

Watches after it ships

For 24 hours after a fix deploys, Claudear monitors for regressions. Re-opened issues, error rate spikes, similar stack traces — it catches problems before your users do.
